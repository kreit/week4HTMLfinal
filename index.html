Conscription day happens every year in July. It's our version of a registration day.
Every Quadrant has theirown schedule on this day. Our Riders Quadrant is most known for their Conscription Day because they must cross the parapet.
Not everyone that attempts to cross the parapet will survive. Almost 20% of the cadets that attempts the parapet will die. This separates the weak from the strong.

Basgiath is not a place for the weak.

Should you choose to enroll in Basigiath,
you must dedicate your life to your chosen path.
You will be allowed no contact with the outside world during your first year.
Not even your family.
This helps to keep you focused while you adjust.
